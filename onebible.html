One Bible Seminars

We have been traveling to the remote villages and small towns since 2004, holding ONE day Bible Studies for National Leaders. We have gathered from 20 to 100 local Pastors in houses churches and church buildings, of Bamboo, Wood and Brick. We provide a lunch and sometimes some traveling money. It cost FHF about $3 a leader. Most Pastor's have never been o Bible School, so we bring Bible School to them. We have personally taught at least 500 Leaders a year since 2004.
Over the years we have been blessed to have Pastor's come on Shot Term mision trips, reaching a number of Seminars. Thanks, Pastor's George Thomas, Mark Schmidt, Scott Neubauer, Mike Cepela and their supporting Teams.
