Bethel Home for Girls

A Joint Vision of Ministry between Keith and Pastor Sayyon to Empower Young Women to become self-sufficient mature followers of Jesus Christ. Protecting them from Human traffickers words are common in the poor villages.

Pastor Sayyon's father donated an old wood house that needed a lot of repairs. Our two initial Partners helped us to renovate the house to make it a Safe Home, we will always be grateful to Watershed Church and Hope Expeditions for gifts. Each night the girls join in Bible study and Worship. On weekends they go home to see their families and help with the 4-14 Children's Club near them. Most Girls are completing their high school or University Education while living at Bethel. Families are able to give some support but are in need of Scholarships, about $35 a month is needed per girl.
