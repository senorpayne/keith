4-14 Children's Club

A fun Club for poor children living in mostly remote villages. They have little or no church near them. Most Clubs are in Villages where there are no churches. Child must have fun, we teach them the Bible Stories, pray and point them to Jesus. Parents are asked to send their children, all National Leaders respect the parents by not "taking " children to Club without permission. Most leaders are older children and Teens that have be apart of Club. It cost about $35 per month for each Club of 30 children. Monthly Sponsorships are needed. What are the cost, snacks, travel, support to Local Leaders, most Teen leaders volunteer.
