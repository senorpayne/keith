About Us

Our personal journey to Southeast Asia began in 2004 and yet it has taken a lifetime in some ways as God and his mercy has granted us faith to believe in His Son Jesus as our only Savior. This journey of Faith has taken us from Youth Ministry in the early 80's to Bible College to holding different leadership roles over the past 25 years both the US and over seas. WE took many short term missions trips to Eastern Europe and Latin America. Along the way raised four children who are adults and married. We are blessed to be grandparents. We have many life lessons that have reminded us of God's faithfulness and our need to draw closer to our Lord. With the help of some friends we started Fathers House Fellowship in 2006.
