Leadership Center

Transforming their Nation with the Penetrating Gospel Light of Jesus Christ. We equip them to be a light in all areas of their culture, in the Education, Government and Business. We teach them the gathering of the church is to prepare it to Go into the Marketplace with the Gospel. Our main Center is almost finished, we still need a kitchen and another Bathroom.
